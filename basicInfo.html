Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Alchemist
  • Author
  • Paulo Coelho
  • Published
  • The novel was first published in 1988 in Brazil, it was then published in the United States by HarperOne.
  • Genre
  • Fiction, Adventure.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set some time in the past though it is not specified. It takes place in various locations, particularly Spain, and the city Tarifa, also the city of Tangier which is located in Northern Africa, and the novel visits the Sahara Desert as well.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is written in the third person point of view, however it does focus primarily on one character, Santiago. The narrator seems to know most of Santiago's inner thoughts and beliefs.
  • Conflict
  • The conflict featured in the novel involves Santiago's journey to reach his personal legend, a vast treasure. He is conflicted as to whether go through with the difficult journey, or to accept the fortune he has already received in life.
