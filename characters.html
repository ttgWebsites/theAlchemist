Characters

  • Santiago
  • Santiago is the protagonist of the novel  He is a determined young shepherd from Andalusia trying to fulfill his Personal Legend of finding his treasure at the foot of the pyramids in Egypt
  • The Alchemist
  • An alchemist that is two hundred years old that lives in the oasis of Al-Fayoum  He is described as wearing only black and riding a white horse  The Alchemist carries a small sword known as a scimitar, the Philosopher’s Stone, and the Elixir of Life  He also has a deep understanding of the importance of Personal Legends
  • Crystal Merchant
  • The Crystal Merchant works in a crystal shop, however his business is very unsuccessful until he meets Santiago  He is a devout Muslim, and a very serious man
  • Englishman
  • The Englishman is a very well educated student of the sciences that wants to learn about alchemy from a real alchemist rather than in just books
  • Melchizedek
  • He is the King of Salem and often helps people pursue their Personal Legends through the use of his “magical powers”
  • Gypsy
  • The Gypsy is an old woman that lives in Tarifa that interprets dreams  She also reads palms and uses black magic, however, ironically she also believes in Christ
  • Camel Driver
  • The Camel Driver is a former orchard owner that is also a devout Muslim  He is a very optimistic man despite past downfalls in his life
  • The Tribal Chieftain of Al-Fayoum
  • A very mean, heartless, and strict tribal leader  The Chieftain Lives in great luxury  He states that Al-Fayoum is a neutral ground
  • Merchant Daughter
  • Just as her name describes, she is the daughter of a merchant, one that purchases Santiago’s wool  She is very beautiful and smart and Santiago is attracted to her
  • The Monk
  • He is a very kind and wise monk that lives near the Egyptian pyramids in a monastery
  • Merchant
  • The Merchant buys Santiago’s wool yearly  Makes sure that Santiago shears the sheep in front of him
  • Santiago’s Father
  • He is a very supportive father that despite its lowly role, is fine with his son being a Shepard  However, deep down, he had longed for Santiago to be a priest
